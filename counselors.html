Know Your Counselors

Who We Are

My wife and I have dedicated ourselves to preparing the younger generation for marriage through coaching and mentoring.

Our Story

With years of wisdom and experience, we both understand the challenges couples face today, especially as many in the newer generation lose sight of the foundational values rooted in a Christian Judeo worldview. Recognizing this growing need, we created this workbook that not only equips couples for a strong and lasting marriage but also serves as a guide for those who are already married but struggling with challenges.

Our Values

  • Restoring the biblical foundation of marriage
  • Emphasizing commitment, love, and the roles God designed
  • Walking alongside couples with faith, wisdom, and practical tools
  • Building marriages on the solid rock of faith
  • Offering hope and direction for a fulfilling and lasting marriage

Our Mission

Whether couples are just beginning their journey or seeking to restore what has been broken, our guidance and biblical wisdom offer hope and direction for a fulfilling and lasting marriage.
